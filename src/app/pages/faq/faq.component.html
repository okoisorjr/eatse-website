How does the service fee work?

If you prefer us to bring our cleaning materials, our crew member will come with a Eatse Cleaning Kit containing the following items: Cleaning Products Floor cleaner Toilet cleaner Multi-purpose cleaner Glass cleaner Cream cleaner Wood furniture polish Cleaning Equipment Vacuum cleaner Mop Bucket & spinner Microfiber cloth Sponge cloth Scouring pad Note: There will be an extra AED 10 per hour charge if you request the cleaning materials.
