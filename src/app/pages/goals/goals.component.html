How Eatse is making a difference with your help

Being a technology company that serves as the bridge between easers and you. Eatse is motivated by WHY's that serves as a vision for the company, these WHY's are intertwined with the SDGs which is a call to end poverty, hunger and to ensure that people enjoy peace and prosperity.

No Poverty

Too many people are still struggling for the most basic human needs and this is as a result of low payment with no access to good food, clean water and a clean environment. Eatse aims at reducing poverty by providing her easers with a sustainable solution by creating a better-payment system thereby making them comfortable and able to provide for their families. According to research, 45% of easers have more than 4 dependents. These dependents live solely off what is paid to easers and as such, Eatse aims at raising the pay for her easers and a click/order from you makes it possible for them to have a stable source of income

Zero Hunger

The SDG aims to eradicate hunger and malnutrition, knowing fully well that hunger leads to heinous crimes. Eatse in turn ensures that easers are taken care of and have no reason to be hungry.
According to research, 73% spend most of their money on their family. Eatse not only pays easers a very good rate but also has a food bank system where foodstuffs can be accessed by all easers.

Good health and well-being

Good health is essential to sustainable development and life expectancy is based on the degree to which good health and well-being is upheld. Eatse cares about the well-being of everyone, therefore, offers free medical consultations to all easers; Eatse in its quest to creating healthcare services around easers aims at establishing a physical lab where lab tests can be accessed.
